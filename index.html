About Me

My name is Sarah Doran. I graduated from Winthrop University with a degree in elementary education and have been living in Charlotte for 5 years. Upon graduating I was hired as a teacher assistant and eventually ended up where I am now- teaching elementary school computer science. Teaching elementary school computer science has given me many experiences over the past 3 years, but one experience that helped open the door to a career change was when I began teaching my students how to code.

Teaching code led me to dream of becoming something I call a “code wizard”. The feeling of creating something out of nothing was thrilling; it was the challenge and reward I had been looking for, but never had the chance to experience. Although my teaching career has been an amazing experience, a career that lends itself to something more creative and challenging has beckoned me to strive for change.

I'm a hard working, overly organized, and persistent individual who wants to make a difference. Positive and enthusiastic, but swift and driven- I'm always looking for ways to make things simpler.

When I am not teaching I am cooking and experimenting with vegetarian meals, visiting local breweries, collecting pins, patches and artwork, and watching "B" horror movies.
