About Me


My name is Sarah Doran. I am an elementary school technology teacher who dreams of becoming a code wizard. I have taught for three years, but have been in schools for almost five years. Although the teaching scene has been good to me, I strive to do something more creative and challenging for my future career.


When I am not teaching I am cooking and experimenting with vegetarian meals, visiting local breweries, collecting pins, patches and artwork, and watching "B" horror movies.
